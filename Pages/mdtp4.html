4  THE IMPORTANCE IF VALUES, ATTITUDES AND BEHAVIOUR IN COUNSELLING (SPECIFIC OUTCOME 4)

 

Specific outcome 4: The role of values, attitudes and behaviour in counselling

SO4AC1

The relationship between own values and attitudes and behaviour is explored with reference to the implications for the counselling. 

SO4AC2

The importance of an attitude of respect for diversity of values is explained with reference to the counselling process

Learning outcomes

At the end of this specific outcome, learners should be able to:

  • Explain the relationship between own values and attitudes and behaviour with reference to the implications for the counselling. 
  • Describe the importance of an attitude of respect for diversity of values with reference to the counselling process.
