3  THE PRINCIPLES AND PROCESS OF COUNSELLING (SPECIFIC OUTCOME 3)

 

Specific outcome 3: Explain the principles and processes of counselling.

SO3:AC1

Generally accepted principles of counselling are explained with reference to their application in different contexts.

SO3:AC2

A counselling process is explained with reference to planning and preparation, identifying and prioritising needs, management, initiation, contracting, terminating, and relating to clients.

SO3:AC3

Techniques and skills required for counselling are explained with reference to when each is appropriate

Learning outcomes:

At the end of this specific outcome the learners will be able to:

  • Discuss generally accepted principles of counselling with reference to their application in different contexts.
  • Explain the counselling process with reference to planning and preparation, identifying and prioritising needs, management, initiation, contracting, terminating, and relating to clients.
  • Demonstrate techniques and skills required for counselling with reference to when each is appropriate
