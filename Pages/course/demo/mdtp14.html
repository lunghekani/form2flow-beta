Item Number: 14

 

Item Name:

GENERAL INSPECTION 

Item Description:

Walk around the machine and look for leaks, damage, loose parts, etc. Retighten the exhaust system bolts after the first 6 to 10 hours of operation.

Activity:

Walk around the machine and look for leaks, damage, loose parts, etc.

Tools:

Tools as required.

Safe Operating Procedures:

Ensure the engine is shut down and locked out as per Mine Procedure.

Comments:

Ensure all sources of stored energy are de-energized before performing any work on equipment.
