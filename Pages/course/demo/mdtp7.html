Item Number: 7

Item Name:

FUEL TANK

Item Description:

Check the fuel level, and fill as required.

Activity:

  1. Move the lockout switch to the ON position. 
  2. Move Engine power switch to the ON position. 
  3. Check the fuel level on the gauge. 
  4. If extra fuel is required, switch off the ignition.
  5. Open fuel tank port.  
  6. Add fuel if needed. 
  7. Replace port cap.

Tools:

  1. Fuel dispenser.

Comments:

Mines Refueling Policies and Procedures to be followed at all times.
