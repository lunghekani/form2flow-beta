Item Number: 12

 

Item Name:

FIRE EXTINGUISHER AND FIRE SUPPRESSION SYSTEM

Item Description:

Check pressure gauge. Indicator should be in the Green zone. Check Expiry date and safety seals.

Activity:

Examine fire-extinguisher as per Mine Procedure.

Tools:

Visual inspection.

Safe Operating Procedures:

  1. Check seals
  2. Check indicator.
  3. Check expiry date.
  4. Examine fire-extinguisher as per Mine Procedure.

Comments:

Only a trained and authorised person may work on a fire suppression system. Refer to Mine Policies and Procedures.
