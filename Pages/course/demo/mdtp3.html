Item Number: 3

Item Name:

FUEL WATER SEPARATOR FILTER

Item Description:

Drain excess water from the fuel-water separators.

Activity:

  1. Undo the small drain plug. 
  2. Open to drain the water. 
  3. Close when clean diesel starts to flow. 
  4. Close, push priming pump a few times to prime.

Tools:

  1. Drip tray.

Safe Operating Procedures:

Dispose of the old diesel in accordance with the regulations.

LC-022-012

Comments:

Always lock out as per Mine Standards and Regulations.
