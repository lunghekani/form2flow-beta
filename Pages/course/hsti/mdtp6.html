Item Number: 6

Item Name:

ENGINE OIL

Item Description:

The engine oil level must be checked, and oil added as required.
Note: Oil filters and oil must be changed, and valve clearance adjusted after the first 50 hours as per Deutz schedule.

Activity:

  1. Open the engine compartment cover. 
  2. Remove the dipstick. 
  3. Clean the dipstick. 
  4. Reinsert the dipstick.  
  5. Remove and examine.  
  6. Add oil if needed.

Tools:

  1. 15W40 Oil can. 
  2. Clean Rag.

Safe Operating Procedures:

Ensure the engine is shut down and locked out as per Mine Procedure.

Comments:

Ensure the oil shows on the full mark of the dipstick.
