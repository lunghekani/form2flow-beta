Item Number: 8

 

Item Name:

HYDRAULIC OIL

 

Item Description:

Check level. Fill as required. (Main hydraulic tank)

 

Activity:

  1. Insert pipe into oil vat.  
  2. Engage oil pump. 
  3. Move the lockout switch to the ON position.  
  4. Switch Engine on at the ignition. 
  5. Open engine compartment cover. 
  6. Press and hold pump start button. 
  7. Visually check the oil level on the gauge untill full. 
  8. Release Start / Stop button. 
  9. Switch Engine OFF at the ignition. 
  10. Close Engine compartment cover. 
  11. Remove the oil filling pipe from the quick coupler and store in the designated area.

 

Tools:

  1. Filling pipe with quick coupler.

 

Comments:

Follow the Mine Filling Procedure.

Use 68 grade hydraulic oil only.
