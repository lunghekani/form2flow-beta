Item Number: 10

 

Item Name:

TYRES

Item Description:

Check condition and pressure. Wheel nut torque dry is 200Nm Tyre pressure is 80 psi (552 kpa).

Activity:

  1. Connect the tyre inflator to the tyre valve. 
  2. Read preasure and inflate or deflate as  required.

Tools:

  1. Torque wrench.
  2. Tyre inflator.

Safe Operating Procedures:

Only a trained and authorised person may work with tyre assemblies. Refer to Mine Policies and Procedures.
