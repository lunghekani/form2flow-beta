S.Q.T.D. - LP DUAL BOOM DRILL AND SUPPORT RIG

 

PREVENTATIVE MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE

 

Safety:

          ONLY TRAINED AUTHORIZED PERSONNEL SHALL OPERATE OR SERVICE THIS MACHINE.

 

Item Number: 1

Item Name:

LOCKOUT AND TAGOUT

Item Description:

Before any work can be done on a machine, a lockout and tagout must be performed.

Activity:

  1. Remove the lock. 
  2. Move the switch to the OFF position. 
  3. Lock the position. 
  4. Lights will turn off when in the OFF position.

 

Tools:

  1. Lockout lock.
  2. Tag.

Safe Operating Procedures:

WARNING! Remember to de-energize all sources of stored energy. Isolate, Tag and lock out. 
LC-022-010

Comments:

Always lock out as per Mine Standards and  Procedures.
