Item Number: 11

Item Name:

WHEEL NUTS

Item Description:

Tighten daily to 200 Nm after the first 50 hours of operation, and every 250 hours of operation thereafter. Ensure nut tightness indicators are fitted. 

Activity:

  1. Torque each wheel nut to specification.
  2. Ensure that the wheel nuts are tightened overcrossed. 

Tools:

  1. Torque wrench.
  2. 27mm Half inch drive socket.

Safe Operating Procedures:

Refer to Mine Policies and Procedures.

Comments:

Only a trained and authorised person may work with tyre assemblies. Refer to Mine Policies and Procedures.
