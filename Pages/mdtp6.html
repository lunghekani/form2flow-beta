6  REFLECTING ON THE COUNSELLING PROCESS (SPECIFIC OUTCOME 6)

Specific outcome 6. Reflecting on the counselling process

SO6AC1

The strengths and limitations of the counselling process are identified and motivated based on personal reflection.

SO6AC2

Key lessons are identified and changes for future counselling sessions are discussed as part of ongoing professional development.

SO6AC3

Personal and organisational strategies to reduce the risk of burnout and secondary trauma are explored with reference to own life and person.

SO6AC4

The concept of supervision is explained and an indication is given of the benefits of supervision for the counsellor.

Learning outcomes

After completion of this specific outcome, learners should be able to:

  • Explain the strengths and limitations of the counselling process.
  • Explain key lessons and changes for future counselling sessions as part of ongoing professional development.
  • Explain burnout and secondary trauma
  • Demonstrate personal and organisational strategies to reduce the risk of burnout and secondary trauma with reference to own life and person
  • Discuss the concept of supervision and give an indication of the benefits of supervision for the counsellor.
